输入：
    images_{t-k:t}
    sparse LiDAR_{t-k:t}
    camera intrinsics/extrinsics
    poses

VGGT 魔改版：
    输出 depth-head feature F_img: [B,S,N,D]
    输出 depth prediction depth: [B,S,H,W]

Step 1: depth downsample
    depth_patch: [B,S,Hp,Wp]

Step 2: image token back-projection
    对每个 patch center (u,v):
        p_cam = depth_patch * K^{-1} [u,v,1]
        p_target_lidar = T_target_lidar_cam_s @ p_cam

Step 3: voxel scatter
    把 F_img[b,s,h,w] scatter 到 target voxel grid
    得到 F_img_voxel: [B,D_i,X,Y,Z]
    得到 img_mask/count: [B,1,X,Y,Z]

Step 4: LiDAR voxel branch
    多帧 LiDAR 变换到 target LiDAR frame
    voxel encoder 得到 F_lidar_voxel: [B,D_l,X,Y,Z]

Step 5: voxel fusion
    F = concat(F_img_voxel, F_lidar_voxel, img_mask, lidar_mask)
    F = Conv3D/FusionBlock(F)

Step 6: occupancy decoder
    logits = decoder(F)
    logits: [B,C,X,Y,Z]

Step 7: loss
    CE / weighted CE / Lovasz / occ BCE
    target: [B,X,Y,Z]
